Handyman Services FAQ

What home repair services can be grouped together?

Drywall repair, fixture replacement, ceiling fan installation, door repair, trim work, shelving, TV mounting, cabinet adjustments, and punch-list maintenance can often be organized into one handyman repair list.

Do you help with property inspection repairs?

Yes. Property inspection repair services may include visible drywall, fixture, door, trim, hardware, cabinet, shelving, and general maintenance items that need to be handled before a sale, rental, or walkthrough.

Can you help with water damage or ceiling repair?

We can help with visible ceiling repair and drywall finish work after the source of the water issue has been identified and handled by the appropriate professional.

Do you handle rental turnover repairs?

Yes. Rental turnover repairs may include drywall repair, fixture replacement, door repair, shelving, trim, cabinet hardware, flooring touch-ups, and general home repair items between tenants or guests.

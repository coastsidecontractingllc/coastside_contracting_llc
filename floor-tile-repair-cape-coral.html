Floor & Tile Work We Handle

Tile Repair and Loose Tile Replacement

Loose tile can make a floor feel unfinished and may point to movement beneath the surface. We help with tile repair and loose tile replacement when individual areas need attention, focusing on a clean repair approach that blends as well as the existing materials allow.

Cracked Tile Repair

Cracked tile can stand out immediately in a kitchen, bathroom, hallway, lanai, or rental property. Cracked tile repair may include removing damaged pieces, addressing visible surface issues, and replacing tile where a repair is practical based on available materials and the condition of the surrounding floor.

Grout Repair

Worn, missing, or damaged grout can make otherwise usable tile look tired. Grout repair helps improve the appearance of tiled areas and can be part of a larger floor and tile repair plan for Cape Coral homes, bathrooms, kitchens, and rental maintenance projects.

Floor Leveling and Subfloor Preparation

Good flooring starts with preparation. Floor leveling and subfloor preparation help create a better surface for flooring installation, LVP installation, laminate flooring, or tile replacement when the visible floor needs more than a simple cosmetic repair.

LVP Installation

Luxury vinyl plank is a common choice for Southwest Florida homes because it can provide a clean look and practical daily use. We help with LVP installation where the space is ready for new flooring, including rooms, rental units, and larger flooring updates.

Laminate Flooring Repair and Transitions

Laminate flooring repair and transition work can help connect rooms, reduce awkward edges, and clean up worn areas. Flooring transitions are especially important where tile, LVP, laminate, or other surfaces meet and the finished detail needs to look intentional.

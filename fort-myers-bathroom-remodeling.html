Fort Myers Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

Do you handle partial bathroom remodels?

Yes. Fort Myers bathroom remodeling can include focused improvements such as vanity updates, bathroom fixture replacement, tile repair, drywall repair, flooring, and finish work.

Can you help with rental bathroom repairs?

Yes. Rental bathroom repairs may include fixtures, trim, caulking, drywall touch-ups, flooring repairs, vanity hardware, and other punch-list items before a tenant or guest arrives.

Do you repair bathroom tile?

Yes. Bathroom tile repair and shower-area surface work can be part of a bathroom repair or remodeling project depending on the condition of the tile and surrounding materials.

Can bathroom drywall repair be included?

Yes. Drywall repair, paint, and finish work can be included after the source of any moisture issue has been identified and addressed.
